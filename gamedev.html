August 6th 2019

Getting basic character class setup, referencing the senior project I worked on with Chris Gix and Connor Alvin check it out here. Also remade my website and setting up the Game Development blog on it to track progress. I talked to Chris too about our last project to avoid some declaration issues we ran into at one point. He said we fixed the circular dependency problem by only including other classes in the .cpp files, not the .h files for classes (which makes sense).

A big aim for me in this game is to allow players the ability to customize their characters play style. I'm looking to have growth percentage rates like fire emblem, this was characters have the potential to gain stats in any category but are more or less likely to get certain stats on level ups. I also want to add a profession/class system to the game that modifies characters growth rates. I'm thinking there might be different tiers of classes. Also, I want players to be able to buy skills/spells with some kind of currency they accumulate from performing certain tasks. I was thinking as players use certain weapons or spells they gain points for that area that they can spend on new skills. I set up some stats today with all this in mind, I'll write more about them as I become more confident in them.
